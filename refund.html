Tiyeni Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

At Tiyeni, we strive to provide a seamless and reliable ride-hailing experience. However, we understand that sometimes issues may arise that require a refund. This policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

  • Cancelled Rides:
    • If a driver cancels your ride after acceptance, you will receive a full refund (if pre-paid) or no charge will apply.
    • If you cancel a ride after a certain time window (e.g., 3 minutes after booking), a cancellation fee may apply unless the driver is significantly delayed.
  • Incorrect Charges: If you were charged incorrectly due to a technical error or fare discrepancy, please report the issue within 24 hours, and we will investigate.
  • Service Issues: If the ride experience was unsatisfactory (e.g., unsafe driving, vehicle not as described), you may request a refund or partial refund.
  • Duplicate Payments: If you were charged multiple times for the same ride, we will refund the extra charges upon verification.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

  • If you cancel a ride repeatedly without a valid reason.
  • If the fare difference is due to route changes requested by you.
  • If the ride was completed as agreed, and no service issues were reported.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Go to "Your Trips" in the Tiyeni app.
  • Select the ride in question.
  • Tap "Report an Issue" and choose the refund reason.
  • Provide any necessary details (e.g., screenshots, descriptions).
